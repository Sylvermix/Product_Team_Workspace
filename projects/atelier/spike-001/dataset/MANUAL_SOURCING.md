# Manual Photo Sourcing — SPIKE-001 Dataset

The download script handles ~55 photos (Unsplash + Pexels). The remaining ~45 are sourced manually.

---

## Remaining quota by source

| Source | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Wikimedia Commons | ~15 | Fashion photography category — verify license per photo |
| Your network (consented) | ~10 | Real phone photos: mirror selfies, street, hauls. **Get explicit consent.** |
| Synthetic (generated) | ~10 | Fill gaps in underrepresented categories |
| Editorial / runway | ~10 | Internal evaluation only — do NOT redistribute |

---

## Naming convention

Files go in `dataset/photos/`. Name them `P056.jpg` through `P100.jpg` (continuing after the downloaded batch).

After adding each file, add its entry to `dataset_manifest.yaml` (copy the schema at the top of that file) and set `ready_for_eval: true`.

## Diversity gaps to fill manually

Check these categories are covered after the automatic download. Fill gaps with manual sourcing.

### Garment categories (target)
- [ ] Tops: 25 total (t-shirt, shirt, blouse, jacket, coat)
- [ ] Bottoms: 20 total (trousers, jeans, skirt, shorts)
- [ ] Full-body: 15 total (dress, jumpsuit, suit)
- [ ] Footwear: 15 total (trainers, boots, heels, sandals)
- [ ] Accessories: 15 total (bag, hat, scarf, sunglasses)
- [ ] Outerwear: 10 total (puffer, trench, blazer)

### Photo types (target)
- [ ] Flat lay: 15
- [ ] On-body editorial/styled: 25
- [ ] Street style / candid: 25
- [ ] Mirror selfie: 20
- [ ] E-commerce product shot: 15

### Hard cases (mandatory — 15 photos)
At least 15 photos must be "hard cases":
- Traditional or cultural garments (kimono, saree, dashiki, etc.)
- Heavily patterned / printed items
- Heavily layered looks
- Low-light or mixed lighting
- Non-Western editorial

### Bias checks before finalising
- [ ] No single source contributes >40% of photos
- [ ] Western fashion editorial capped at 50%
- [ ] At least 20 photos with plus-size or non-normative body types
- [ ] Fitzpatrick scale I–VI distributed across on-body shots
- [ ] Gender: masc ~35%, femme ~40%, androgynous/NB ~15%, children/teen ~10%

For every photo from someone in your network, record:

```
Name: _______________
Date: _______________
Consent: "I consent to my photo being used for internal AI product evaluation at Atelier. The photo will not be published or redistributed."
Signature / initials: ___
```

Store consent records separately — not in this repo.

## Synthetic photos (Stable Diffusion)

Use any SD model with a fashion LoRA. Suggested prompts for underrepresented categories:

- `plus-size woman wearing floral midi dress, street style, natural light`
- `man wearing traditional dashiki, editorial portrait`
- `androgynous person wearing oversized trench coat, urban background`
- `woman wearing hijab and blazer, office setting, natural light`
- `low-light bar scene, leather jacket, moody atmosphere`

These have no license restrictions — generated by us for internal use.

## After all 100 photos are in place

**Lock ground truth BEFORE running any model.**

1. Open `dataset_ground_truth.yaml`
2. Fill in every entry: category, color_family, style_register, price_tier, notes
3. Add your initials and today's date to each entry
4. Tag the dataset: `git tag dataset-v1.0`
5. Only then run `python -m evaluation.run_eval`
